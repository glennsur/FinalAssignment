GOAL 12

Responsible consumption and production

The world today is facing a lot of problems. Some of them are: global warming, hunger, waste, and the tomorrow's problem of overpopulation. Fortunately, throwing away less food can combat all of those three problems at once. If less food if being throw away, less food can be produced and more people can be fed. Furthermore, temporary waste storages could be used for waste like plastic or other materials.

We consumers does also have an active role in the production cycle. One of them can both help you to save money on food and save the planet. That is to stop wasting food. Globally, 1300 kilograms of food is thrown away each year.
